About me

Background

Born and raised in Germany near Munich, I graduated high school in 2016. Successful in track and field (decathlon), I took on a scholarship opportunity to study in the United States. Completing my freshman year in Milwaukee, I transferred to the University of Arkansas at Little Rock in 2017. I graduated with a Bachelor in Business and Economics and Finance in May 2020 and I am on track to finish my Bachelor in Business Analytics in May 2021.
