About my High School Education

My high school’s education was focused on the humanities and offered a wide variety of classes. However, the focus was clearly on languages and social sciences but not limited to those.

Most memorably, my school offered amazing extracurricular activities that taught me valuable lessons for life. In one of my classes, we crossed the alps with mountain bikes (8 days) which required rigorous planning, endurance and technique practice over the course of two years. It has taught me exceptional interpersonal and team working skills and the importance of collaboration.
